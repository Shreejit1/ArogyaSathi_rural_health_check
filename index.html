🔬 What is PCOD / PCOS?

PCOD (Polycystic Ovarian Disease) is a hormonal condition where the ovaries produce immature eggs that form cysts. It affects 1 in 5 women in India — including in rural areas — but is often undiagnosed. Early detection makes a huge difference.

Common in Ages 15–40

PCOD is most common in reproductive-age women. It is NOT a disease you caused — it is hormonal and can run in families.

Treatable with Lifestyle Changes

For many women, diet, exercise, and stress management dramatically improve PCOD symptoms without heavy medication.
